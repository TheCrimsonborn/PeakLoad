Quick Tips

How accurate are the 1RM formulas?

Each formula excels at different rep ranges. Try a couple and choose the one that best matches your historical bests.

Can I edit warm-up templates?

Select a template that matches the session focus (heavy single, ramp, or volume). Adjust the final percentages if you want more or fewer sets.

Why cap percentages at 120%?

Keeping the range realistic prevents accidental entries that result in unusable loads. You can still use the warm-up planner for supra-maximal work.
